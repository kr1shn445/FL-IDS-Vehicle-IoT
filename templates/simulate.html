Differential Privacy Algorithm
INPUT: Local weights W after training, Clip C=5.0, Noise σ=0.001
STEP 1 — Sensitivity Bounding:
norm = ||W||₂  ←  compute L2 norm of weight tensor
if norm > C: W_clipped = W × (C / norm)  ←  scale down
else: W_clipped = W  ←  no change needed
STEP 2 — Gaussian Noise Injection:
η ~ N(0, σ²C²)  ←  sample calibrated Gaussian noise
W_dp = W_clipped + η  ←  add noise
OUTPUT: W_dp sent to server instead of raw W
